HTML

Hyper Text Markup Language

HTML Elements Categories

HTML elements or tags can be broadly categorized based on their functionality and purpose. Understanding these categories can help you structure your HTML documents effectively and choose the appropriate elements for different parts of your webpage

  1. Structural Elements:

    These elements define the structure of the webpage, such as headings, paragraphs, lists, and divisions.

    h1 to h6: Headings

    p tag: Paragraph

    ul, ol, li Unordered and Ordered Lists

    div: Division or section of the document

    Semantic structural elements introduced in HTML5 : header, footer, nav, main, section, article
